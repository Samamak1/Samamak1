## How I run programs

1. **Frame** the business problem, stakeholders, baseline, and evidence of success.
2. **Map** workstreams, owners, milestones, dependencies, constraints, and the critical path.
3. **Align** stakeholders around priorities, tradeoffs, scope, and decision rights.
4. **Govern** risks, issues, decisions, KPIs, and escalations with a clear cadence.
5. **Adopt** through training, documentation, reinforcement, and operating ownership.
6. **Measure** the result, close control gaps, capture learning, and institutionalize what works.
