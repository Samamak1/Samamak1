## How I run programs

| Phase | Leadership practice |
|---:|---|
| **01. Frame** | Define the business problem, stakeholders, baseline, and evidence that will count as success. |
| **02. Map** | Translate the outcome into workstreams, owners, milestones, dependencies, constraints, and a realistic critical path. |
| **03. Align** | Surface tradeoffs early and make priorities, scope choices, and decision rights legible. |
| **04. Govern** | Keep risks, issues, decisions, KPIs, and escalations visible to teams and leaders. |
| **05. Adopt** | Pair delivery with training, documentation, reinforcement, and clear operating ownership. |
| **06. Measure** | Compare results with the baseline, close control gaps, capture learning, and institutionalize what works. |
